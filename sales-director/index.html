Sales director for precise results

Sales directors need to be natural leaders who can unlock the potential of your target market and the capabilities of your sales division through expert planning and management. Further to that, they need to be completely in tune with your brand and your products or services, and capable of building the tailored frameworks and processes for your teams to succeed. While there are many talented sales directors out there, it can be almost impossible to find the professional you’re looking for when you have a role to fill. 4Twenty can provide access to the best of those sales directors wherever your business is located in Australia. Not just limited to Sydney and Melbourne, our consultants can identify leading sales talent in all regions and for all industries.

Find greater talent with our careful selection of candidates

Rather than suggesting job seekers for the sake of numbers as others may, our entire recruitment strategy is centred on only providing people who are perfect for the role. 4Twenty’s consultants originated in the very industry that they now recruit for, which gives us a familiar perspective of the passion, expertise and leadership skills required in great sales directors. We source talent from our growing database and using innovative marketing techniques, then conduct in-depth screening interviews to obtain an accurate picture of candidates’ personalities, behavioural qualities and strengths. By the time we present a selection of capable candidates from 4Twenty for the specific role you need filled, anyone lacking the required motivation or experience has been filtered out – out leaving only exceptional sales and management professionals with the expertise and desire to join your team.
