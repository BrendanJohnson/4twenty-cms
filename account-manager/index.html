Account Manager

Simplifying your account manager process

Whether you’re in media, retail or marketing, your account managers play a vital role in connecting clients to the creative and strategic solutions they need. 4Twenty Consulting offers dedicated sales recruitment services right across Australia, making it easy for you to find the account managers you need through carefully curated shortlists of pre-screened professionals. Our sales background puts us in good stead to understand your requirements and to reach out to the right job seekers in order to fill your roles. 4Twenty consultants specialise in finding those talented junior professionals who are likely to rise through the ranks to become your biggest assets. At every level of every expertise, in every city of Australia, 4Twenty can keep your team equipped with the right people when you need them.

We’ll find the right fit for your work culture

At 4Twenty our existing candidate network is constantly refreshed with new talent from leading universities across the country as well as our quarterly seminars and targeted advertising strategies. It is this diverse job seeking network that we access when it comes to identifying the right account managers for your roles. Every potential candidate is assessed through a phone and face-to-face interview process with our consultants, and only the top level professionals make it through to the final selection. We encourage our candidates to then express their preferred brands and business types which ensures that the employees you bring into your company are both equipped and driven from the very beginning.

4Twenty is your solution for all sales recruitment

From junior account managers starting their careers to experienced and specialised account managers for niche industries, our consultants are available to help you fill every role you need filled in your sales team. Contact us now on email us at info@4twentyconsulting.com and ask about how our account manager recruitment services can help to grow your business.
