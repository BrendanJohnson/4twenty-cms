Australia-wide team leader

Talented team leaders are always in demand. How do you secure the top-level employees for your own business? A specialist sales recruitment service can provide the connection you need. 4Twenty’s consultants possess years of experience in the sales world, and our recruitment focuses purely on finding the professionals you need to drive your team to success and growth. What sets our candidates above others?

4Twenty has a higher average hiring rate than many of our competitors, meaning you can expect a selection of better suited candidates for every role.
